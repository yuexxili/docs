## Electrical Components

This model includes: Power Lock, Alarm, DC-DC converter, Horn, U3LTE ECU, Harness assembly, Lamp Controller, Lock Controller Unit, Electronic Handlebar Lock.

<Note>
  Dual battery version only: Black and white Display is included.
</Note>

## Known Failure Points

- Electronic handlebar lock — see [Locks & Security](/repair/locks-security)
- ECU (U3LTE) faults — see [Electrical & Controller](/repair/electrical-controller)
- Hydraulic disc brake failure — see [Brakes](/repair/brakes)
- Motor overload — see [Motor & Drivetrain](/repair/motor-drivetrain)

<Warning>
  If the issue is not resolved after following repair procedures, [escalate to after-sales](/escalation).
</Warning>
