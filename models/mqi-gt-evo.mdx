## Disassembly Sections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Section 1 – Front Body Lower Lip">
    Pull out gently and directly. Remove two bottom screws then pull out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 2 – Front Body Upper">
    Remove front body upper panel by unclipping retaining hooks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 3 – Dashboard">
    Remove dashboard cover block before accessing display connections.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 4 – Seat and Plug Lock Bracket">
    Remove six screws then pull out seat. Remove two screws to detach plug lock bracket.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 5 – Fan and Handrail">
    Remove 4 screws to detach fan. Disconnect fan controller connector. Remove four hexagon flange bolts then pull out handrail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 6 – Central Guard Panel and FOC">
    Remove two screws for central guard panel plate. Remove six screws for central guard panel. Remove FOC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 7 – Side Stand and Side Stand Switch">
    Unscrew and unplug side stand switch. Unscrew side stand screw to remove.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 8 – Front and Rear Fender">
    Remove two hexagon flange bolts on front fender. Remove two cross recessed medium screws on rear fender.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 9 – Motor">
    See [Motor & Drivetrain](/repair/motor-drivetrain) for full procedure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Known Failure Points

- FOC (Field Oriented Controller) faults — see [Electrical & Controller](/repair/electrical-controller)
- Hydraulic disc brake failure — see [Brakes](/repair/brakes)
- Rear shock absorber failure — see [Suspension](/repair/suspension)
- Battery pack faults (red flash = fault status) — see [Battery & BMS](/repair/battery-bms)

<Warning>
  If the issue is not resolved after following repair procedures, [escalate to after-sales](/escalation).
</Warning>
