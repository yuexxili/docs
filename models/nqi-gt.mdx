## Disassembly Sections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Section 1 – Windshield">
    Unscrew two screws on the windshield to remove.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 2 – Front Neck Cover">
    Remove two front central cover screws. Lift up front neck cover to remove.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 3 – Front Panel">
    Unscrew two screws on top of front panel. Take out two push-in rivets. Remove front panel by pushing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 4 – Under Seat Storage Compartment">
    Unscrew four screws. Lift up storage compartment. Pull battery compartment lock cable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 5 – Rear Central Cover">
    Unscrew left and right side screws. Pull out rubber cover and unscrew. Pull out rear central cover carefully.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Section 6 – Battery Compartment Cover and Footrest">
    Pull out footrest plug and unscrew. Unscrew four cross recessed pan head screws. Unscrew two hexagon flange bolts. Unscrew left and right side screws.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sections 7–20 – Remaining Panels, Brakes, Motor">
    Refer to category repair guides for brake system (Section 17), side stand (Section 18), fenders (Section 19), and motor (Section 20).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Known Failure Points

- HALL failure — see [Motor & Drivetrain](/repair/motor-drivetrain)
- MOSFET failure — see [Electrical & Controller](/repair/electrical-controller)
- Hydraulic disc brake failure — see [Brakes](/repair/brakes)

<Warning>
  If the issue is not resolved after following repair procedures, [escalate to after-sales](/escalation).
</Warning>
