## Disassembly Sections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sections 1–6 – Body Panels">
    Follow same sequence as NQi GT: Windshield → Front Neck Cover → Front Panel → Under Seat → Rear Central Cover → Battery Compartment Cover.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sections 7–20 – Remaining Panels, Brakes, Motor">
    Refer to category repair guides for brake system (Section 17), side stand (Section 18), fenders (Section 19), and motor (Section 20).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Known Failure Points

- HALL failure — see [Motor & Drivetrain](/repair/motor-drivetrain)
- MOSFET failure — see [Electrical & Controller](/repair/electrical-controller)
- Hydraulic disc brake failure — see [Brakes](/repair/brakes)

<Warning>
  If the issue is not resolved after following repair procedures, [escalate to after-sales](/escalation).
</Warning>
