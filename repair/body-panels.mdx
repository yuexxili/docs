## General Rules

<Warning>
  Hooks and claws at various sections must be installed properly during assembly. Never force a panel — if it resists, check for missed screws or clips.
</Warning>

- Always loosen multiple assemblies from outside to inside
- Store parts in installation sequence for reassembly
- Clean all surfaces before reassembly

## Standard Panel Removal Order

Most NIU models follow this sequence. Always check the model-specific page for variations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Windshield (if fitted)">
    Unscrew two screws on the windshield to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Front Neck Cover">
    Remove two front central cover screws. Lift up front neck cover to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Front Panel">
    Unscrew two screws on top of front panel. Take out two push-in rivets. Remove front panel by pushing it outward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under Seat Storage Compartment">
    Unscrew four screws. Lift up storage compartment. Pull battery compartment lock cable if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rear Central Cover">
    Unscrew left and right side screws. Pull out rubber cover and unscrew hidden bolt. Pull out rear central cover carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Battery Compartment Cover and Footrest">
    Pull out footrest plug and unscrew. Unscrew four cross recessed pan head screws. Unscrew two hexagon flange bolts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Side Panels, Handrail, End Caps">
    Continue with remaining covers in order. Refer to model-specific manual for exact sequence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## MQi GT / MQi GT EVO Panel Order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Front Body Lower Lip">Pull out gently and directly. Remove two bottom screws.</Step>
  <Step title="Front Body Upper">Unclip retaining hooks.</Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard">Remove dashboard cover block.</Step>
  <Step title="Seat and Plug Lock Bracket">Remove six screws for seat. Two screws for plug lock bracket.</Step>
  <Step title="Fan and Handrail">4 screws for fan. Disconnect connector. 4 hexagon flange bolts for handrail.</Step>
  <Step title="Central Guard Panel">2 screws for panel plate. 6 screws for panel. Then access BCS/FOC.</Step>
  <Step title="Side Stand">Unscrew and unplug switch. Unscrew side stand bolt.</Step>
  <Step title="Fenders">2 hexagon flange bolts (front). 2 cross recessed screws (rear).</Step>
</Steps>

## Reassembly Notes

- Apply correct torque values when reinstalling bolts (refer to model-specific torque table)
- Ensure all connectors are fully seated before closing panels
- Test all switches and lights after reassembly
