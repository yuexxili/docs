## System Overview

NIU scooters use hydraulic disc brakes front and rear. The system consists of:

- Oil pump body assembly
- Front/Rear brake disc
- Brake cylinder assembly
- Brake pad assembly
- Brake hose assembly
- Hydraulic brake handle

## Inspection Checklist

Before diagnosing a brake fault, check the following:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check brake pads">
    Inspect for wear. Replace if pad thickness is below minimum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check brake disc">
    Inspect for scoring, warping, or cracks. Check disc thickness.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check brake fluid">
    Inspect fluid level in reservoir. Top up if low. Check for leaks along hose and at caliper.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check main brake pump">
    Inspect piston surface and oil cylinder wall for excessive wear. Check piston cup for damages, cracks, or aging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check brake caliper">
    Inspect seal ring for damages, cracks, or aging. Inspect caliper piston surface and oil cylinder wall for excessive wear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Failure Diagnosis

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Brake feels spongy or requires excessive lever pull">
    - Air in the brake line — bleed the brake system
    - Brake fluid level low — top up and check for leaks
    - Worn brake pads — replace pads
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brake squealing or grinding noise">
    - Contaminated brake pads — clean or replace
    - Worn brake pads — replace
    - Warped or scored disc — replace disc
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brake dragging (scooter hard to push)">
    - Caliper piston not retracting — clean or replace caliper
    - Brake hose kinked or collapsed — inspect and replace hose
    - Handbrake cable adjusted too tight (mechanical backup) — readjust
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brake fluid leak">
    - Inspect all joints, banjo bolts, and hose connections
    - Check caliper seal ring — replace if damaged
    - Check main pump piston cup — replace if cracked or aged
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No braking effect">
    - Complete fluid loss — inspect entire system for leaks
    - Brake pads fully worn — replace immediately
    - Hydraulic pump failure — replace pump assembly
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Torque Reference

| Fastener | Torque |
|----------|--------|
| Brake caliper bolt | 4.0 Nm (front) / 4.0 Nm (rear) |
| Brake disc bolt | 3.0 Nm |

<Warning>
  Always use correct brake fluid type. Do not mix fluid types. After any brake system work, test brakes at low speed before returning the scooter to the customer.
</Warning>

<Note>
  If brake failure persists after inspection and repair, [escalate to after-sales](/escalation).
</Note>
