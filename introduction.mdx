## What This Wiki Is For

This wiki is for **authorized NIU dealers and service technicians** responsible for first-line diagnosis and repair of NIU electric scooters sold in the European market.

Use this wiki to:
- Look up repair procedures by **model** or **issue category**
- Reference **diagnostic error codes**
- Understand when and how to **escalate** to NIU after-sales

<Warning>
  This wiki is intended for trained technicians only. Improper repair can cause injury or damage to the scooter. Always follow safety procedures.
</Warning>

## Safety Reminders

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Before you begin any repair">
    - Power off the scooter completely
    - Disconnect the battery before working on electrical systems
    - Use metric tools wherever possible
    - Do not use non-original parts unless confirmed as equivalent
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="During disassembly">
    - Loosen multiple assemblies from outside to inside
    - Store parts in installation sequence for reassembly
    - Remove dirt and oil stains before taking measurements
    - Apply recommended lubricants after reassembly
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="After repair">
    - Test all repaired systems before returning the scooter to the customer
    - Document the repair and any replaced parts
    - If the issue persists, follow the escalation guide
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
